Wings of Fire

Description

"Wings of Fire" is an autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India. It tells the story of his rise from humble beginnings to becoming a key figure in Indian aerospace and missile development. The book provides insights into his early life, education, and his work at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
