A Wrinkle in Time

Description

"A Wrinkle in Time" by Madeleine L'Engle is a science fantasy novel about a young girl named Meg Murry who, along with her brother Charles Wallace and friend Calvin O'Keefe, travels through time and space to rescue her father from an evil force. The novel explores themes of good versus evil, the power of love, and the importance of individuality.
