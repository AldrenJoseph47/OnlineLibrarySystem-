Elon Musk by Walter Isaacson

Description

"Elon Musk" by Walter Isaacson is a comprehensive biography of the visionary entrepreneur and innovator. It delves into Musk's life, from his early days in South Africa to his achievements in technology and space exploration with companies like Tesla and SpaceX. The book provides an in-depth look at Musk's relentless drive, his challenges, and his impact on the future of technology.
