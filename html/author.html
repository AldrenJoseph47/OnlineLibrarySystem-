Meet Our Authors

APJ Abdul Kalam

Renowned Indian scientist and former President of India, known for his contributions to missile development and his inspirational books.

Anne Frank

Author of "The Diary of a Young Girl," which provides a poignant account of her life hiding from the Nazis during World War II.

Amy Brown

Acclaimed author known for her contemporary fiction and engaging storytelling.

Holly Jackson

Best known for her gripping young adult novels, including "A Good Girl's Guide to Murder."

JK Rowling

Author of the globally successful Harry Potter series, known for her fantasy novels and contributions to literature.

Nicholas Sparks

Famous for his romance novels, including "The Notebook" and "A Walk to Remember."

Walter Isaacson

Biographer and author known for his comprehensive biographies of influential figures such as Steve Jobs and Leonardo da Vinci.
