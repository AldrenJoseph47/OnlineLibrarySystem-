War and Peace

Description

"War and Peace" is a novel by the Russian author Leo Tolstoy, first published from 1865 to 1869. It is regarded as one of Tolstoy's finest works and as one of the greatest novels ever written. The novel chronicles the history of the French invasion of Russia and the impact of the Napoleonic era on Tsarist society through the stories of five Russian aristocratic families.
