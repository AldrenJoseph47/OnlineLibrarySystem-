As Good as Dead

Description

"As Good as Dead" by Holly Jackson is the third and final book in the "A Good Girl's Guide to Murder" series. It follows Pip Fitz-Amobi as she deals with the aftermath of her previous investigations while uncovering a new mystery that hits closer to home. The book is a thrilling conclusion to the series, filled with suspense and twists.
